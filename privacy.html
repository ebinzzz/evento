Refund Policy

Our Commitment to Satisfaction

At Aedan Gardens, we are committed to ensuring the satisfaction of our customers with every purchase. If you are not entirely satisfied with your purchase, we're here to help.

Eligibility for Refund

Products Eligible for Refund

  • Plants: Only plants that are in their original condition and packaging can be returned. Plants should not show any signs of damage, disease, or distress.
  • Non-Plant Products: Planters, gardening tools, and other non-plant items can be returned if they are unused and in their original packaging.

Products Not Eligible for Refund

  • Customized or Personalized Products: Items that have been customized or personalized cannot be returned unless they are faulty or damaged.
  • Gift Cards: Gift cards are non-refundable.
  • Sale Items: Only regular-priced items may be refunded. Sale items are non-refundable.

Refund Process

  1. Initiating a Refund: To initiate a refund, please contact our customer service within 7 days of receiving your order. Provide your order number and details of the product you wish to return.
  2. Return: After receiving confirmation from our customer service team, carefully package the item(s) and ship them back to us at the address provided. Customers are responsible for return shipping costs.
  3. Inspection and Approval: Once we receive your returned item, it will be inspected by our quality assurance team. We will notify you of the approval or rejection of your refund.
  4. Refund Credit: If approved, the refund will be processed, and a credit will be automatically applied to your original method of payment within 5-7 working days.

Cancellation Policy

  • Orders Cancelled Within 24 Hours: Orders can be cancelled within 24 hours of purchase. To cancel an order, please contact our customer service team immediately.
  • Orders Cancelled After Shipment: If the cancellation request is received after the order has been shipped, it will be treated as a return, and the customer will be responsible for return shipping costs.

Damaged or Faulty Items

If you receive a damaged or faulty item, please contact our customer service team within 48 hours of receiving your order. We will arrange for a replacement or refund, depending on the situation.
